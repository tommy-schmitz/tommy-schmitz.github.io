About

Control your monsters (each marked with a red flag) and defeat the enemy monsters (each marked with a blue flag)! Click a monster to select it, click a blue square to move there, and click a red square to attack that enemy. Click on your monster after it's moved to wait instead of attacking.

Some monsters have natural affinities for certain terrains, and get bonuses to attack and defense while on those squares. Bunnies have an affinity for forests and minotaurs have an affinity for mountains.

You can move the camera using the w, s, a, and d keys. If you have moved all of your monsters that you can see and nothing is happening, try looking around.

Development

This game was made by Tommy, Shawn, and Eli as part of a game jam that went from Saturday, July 12 to Sunday, July 13, 2014. The basic framework was copied from our previous game jam project, Mia the Mage. Other than that everything in this game was made within those two days.
